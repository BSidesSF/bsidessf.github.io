BSidesSF 2026 will be held March 21-22!

The Call for Participation (CFP) is now open!

We are now accepting submissions for presentations, workshops, and other sessions for BSidesSF 2026. The CFP will remain open until October 14, 2025. To submit your proposal, please visit the CFP submission page.

We're looking forward to bringing you another amazing event in 2026. If you have any feedback about the 2025 event, please be sure to fill in the feedback form

This event would not have been possible without support from our sponsors. We sincerely thank them for being a part of our event and being willing to support us financially. Please take a moment to check them out, especially if you didn't get a chance to talk with them at BSidesSF 2025.

Want to stay in the loop? Join our slack (see the upper right slack icon), or sign up in the box to your right to receive email updates!

Want to be a part of the organizers team that makes this event happen year after year? It's a big responsibility and we'd welcome your aid. Please see our organizers interest page for more information and to apply.

If you have any other questions or comments, don't hesitate to reach out to info [@] bsidessf.org.
