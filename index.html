BSidesSF 2026 is happening, March 21-22!

Registration is Open!

Tickets for BSidesSF 2026 are now available! You can buy your tickets at the usual place: Right here!

Ticket prices will be tiered again this year, starting at $100 early bird pricing until February 19th. On February 20th, the price will raise to $110 (and t-shirts will no longer be available for pre-purchase).

We've published the schedule!

We're excited for the line-up of talks, workshops, birds-of-feather meetings, and meetups that are happening. With more tracks than before, we're providing as much valuable content as we can. After sifting through almost 600 submissions, we hope you'll find topics which interest you and talks that inspire you.

See the full schedule

Note: Workshops require specific registration and have limited space. Register early, before they fill up!

Call For Volunteers is now open!

Every year about 200 volunteers help the BSidesSF staff to make the weekend a smooth experience for everyone. As a volunteer, you'll get to help keeps everything flowing, see some of the behind the scenes work, and support this local BSides event. We're looking for you, and you can sign up in our volunteers portal.

Thank you to our sponsors!

This event would not have been possible without support from our sponsors. We sincerely thank them for being a part of our event and being willing to support us financially. Please take a moment to check them out. You'll get to see them in person at BSidesSF 2026, bring all your questions, they'll be excited to talk with you!

Join Us!

Want to be a part of the team that makes this event happen year after year? It's a big responsibility and we'd welcome your aid. Please see our organizers interest page for more information and to apply.

One of the best ways to show you're committed and want to work with us, become a full-day volunteer in an area. This also allows us to get to know you better, see how you operate, and decide if we want you to join the team.

Want to stay in the loop? Join our slack (see the upper right slack icon), or sign up in the box to your right to receive email updates!

If you have any other questions or comments, don't hesitate to reach out to info [@] bsidessf.org.
