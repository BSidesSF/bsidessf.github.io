Call for 2018 Volunteers

Feb 26, 2018

Interested in helping at BSidesSF 2018?

BSidesSF is a 100% volunteer-run event and simply wouldn’t be possible without the help of our awesome community. If you’re interested in helping, take a look at our volunteer page for current openings. In exchange for at least four hours of your time, you’ll receive free admission and meals during the conference.

If you have any questions, please contact volunteer [@] bsidessf.org or join #volunteers on our Slack.
