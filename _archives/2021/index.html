BSidesSF 2021 is fully virtual this year with our Recharge themed event!

In the spirit of returning and restoring, we've decided to revisit some of the highlights from past events as well as feature a select few new sessions. Over the course of four days we will be hosting two mental health-focused keynotes, four village presentations, and a CTF as well as replaying some awesome past talks! All sessions will be streamed live to our YouTube channel and paired with presenter Q&A facilitated via Reddit.

  • Sunday, March 7th — 12:00-4:00pm PST

  • Tuesday, March 9th — 12:00-2:00pm PST
